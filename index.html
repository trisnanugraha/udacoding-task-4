About Us

TozenTea is the best tea leaves producer in Indonesia. TozenTea was founded in 2020 which always ensures the freshness of tea leaves when they are picked until they are ready for consumption. We continue to create product innovations for processed tea leaves according to the tastes of the Indonesian people.
